Our Story




  • March 2016

    Tasty Beginnings

    Over delightful lunches, Ravi & Annabelle were discussing about impressive technologies they had seen and how they could be further improved upon. The topic of interest eventually became fixated upon a powerful potential device that could enable users to apply deep learning and GPU computing to network traffic.

  • April 2016

    Incorporating SmartCow

    Word started spreading amongst close friends, and the chatter eventually reached the big companies! Within a week, our founders received over 50 requests to build this GPU-accelerated networking device; and that was when they knew they had to start prototype and incorporate SmartCow Pte Ltd.

  • april 2016

    Idea to Manufacturing

    The first prototype was fully functional! At this stage, our founders started working on perfecting the mechanical & electronic designs and sourcing for premium components, to make the Tera+ a quality product that complies to commercial standards. All SmartCow Tera+ units are manufactured with the highest precision in Japan.

  • may 2016

    Transition to Full Service

    With the Tera+ device ready, the SmartCow team started building a custom software. As with most people, they got a little too excited and started developing multiple software kits compatible with those most commonly used developers around the world. All our SDKs are open-source because we want you to experience the same enthusiasm we felt when working with the Tera+, and maximise its full processing capabilities!

Wireshark+

An custom implimention of Wireshark, integreated with Torch Framework.

At SmartCow, we impliented Wireshark for ARM 64bit Platform, integreated with Torch Framework a Machine Learning and Deep Learning framework Torch7 maintained by Ronan, Clément, Koray and Soumith of Facebook.

With wiresharks powerful lua api and torch Deep Learning capablites, users can apply Machine Learning/Deep Learning to network traffic. SmartCow provides training models for network analytics,network behavior analysis, threat detection.

Suricata

Open Source IDS / IPS / NSM engine

Suricata implements a complete signature language to match on known threats, policy violations and malicious behaviour. Suricata will also detect many anomalies in the traffic it inspects. Suricata is capable of using the specialized Emerging Threats Suricata ruleset and the VRT ruleset.GPU acceleration uploads some CPU intensive tasks to your graphics card.
